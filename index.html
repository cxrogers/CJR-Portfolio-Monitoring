Portfolio Monitoring SMS Service

Service Description

Christopher Rogers Investment Services provides automated SMS notifications for personal investment portfolio tracking and monitoring. This service sends real-time alerts about portfolio performance during market trading hours.

What You'll Receive

  • Portfolio Performance Alerts: New daily highs and lows
  • Market Comparison Updates: Performance relative to QQQ and SPY benchmarks
  • System Status Notifications: Critical alerts during automated operation
  • Error Notifications: System issues requiring attention

Message Frequency

You will receive approximately 5-15 messages per trading day during market hours (6:25 AM - 1:30 PM Pacific Time, Monday-Friday). No messages are sent during weekends or market holidays.

SMS Consent Agreement

By using this service, you explicitly consent to receive SMS text messages from Christopher Rogers Investment Services regarding your investment portfolio monitoring.

You understand that:

  • Messages will be sent to the phone number you provided
  • Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile plan
  • This is an automated service for personal portfolio monitoring
  • No marketing or promotional messages will be sent
  • You can opt out at any time using the methods below

Consent is voluntary and you may withdraw consent at any time.

How to Opt Out (Unsubscribe)

To stop receiving SMS messages, you have several options:

  • Reply "STOP" to any SMS message
  • Reply "UNSUBSCRIBE" to any SMS message
  • Email: rogers.cj@gmail.com with "SMS UNSUBSCRIBE" in the subject
  • Contact us using the information below

Opt-out requests will be processed within 24 hours during business days.

Privacy Policy

Your phone number and personal information are used solely for providing this SMS notification service. We do not:

  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Store unnecessary personal information
  • Send unsolicited promotional messages

Your data is protected and will be deleted upon service termination or opt-out request.

Service Hours

SMS notifications are sent during market trading hours:

  • Monday through Friday: 6:25 AM - 1:30 PM Pacific Time
  • Weekends and Holidays: No messages sent
  • After-hours: Critical system alerts only
